Microbial Community Dynamics

Focused on complex microbial communities, the Ottesen lab is in the Department of Microbiology at the University of Georgia. Projects in our lab have focused on topics such as marine and aquatic microbiology, host-microbiome associations, and antibiotic resistance gene ecology.
